Three Converging Mandates — 2026
EU Farm to Fork
2026–2027

Mandatory origin traceability and digital documentary equivalence for all non-EU food imports. VeriPura is the importer-side attestation layer.

EUDR
Dec 2026 · Jun 2027 SMEs

Deforestation due diligence mandatory for cocoa, coffee, soy, palm oil, beef, rubber, timber. DDS required via TRACES before EU/EEA entry.

US FDA FSMA 204
Enforcement Jul 2028

Traceability recordkeeping legally in force. Preparation obligations active now. Dual EU/US compliance accounts are VeriPura's highest-ARPA segment.

Provisionally applying Feb 2026
